Wollies Dog Rescue

Foster-based · Pretoria, Gauteng

Wollies doesn't run a kennel block — it runs a network of foster homes across Pretoria. Every dog in their care sleeps on a couch, not concrete, while they wait to be matched with a permanent family. It's slower than a traditional shelter model, but it means each dog is known, socialised, and never "just a number".

200+Dogs Rehomed
100%Foster-Based
  • 🏠No physical shelter — every dog lives in a vetted foster home until adopted.
  • 🩺Sterilisation, vaccination and basic vet care before any adoption is finalised.
  • 📋Thorough home-checks and matching, so adoptions are built to last.
  • 🚨Responds to street-dog rescues, dumped litters and owner-surrender cases.

Because Wollies is foster-based, donated food and supplies are split across dozens of homes — your gift genuinely stretches further.

🐾

SPCA South Africa

100+ Years · Branches Nationwide

The SPCA is South Africa's oldest and most established animal welfare body — a network of branches that combines a shelter, a low-cost veterinary clinic, an inspectorate, and an education programme under one roof. Where Wollies focuses purely on dogs in foster care, the SPCA's Tshwane branch houses and treats both dogs and cats directly on-site.

100+Years of Service
50+Branches in SA
  • ⚖️A statutory mandate to investigate animal cruelty and enforce welfare law.
  • 🏥Subsidised veterinary clinics for communities who can't afford private care.
  • 🐱On-site shelter housing for both dogs and cats awaiting adoption.
  • 📚Community education on responsible, affordable pet ownership.

Donated supplies go straight into daily shelter operations — feeding, bedding and basic care for animals on-site right now.
